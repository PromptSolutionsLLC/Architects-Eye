P10: Realistic 3D satellite glTF models with LOD switching.

Replace cyan triangles with proper 3D satellite models. Cesium ships free glTF satellite assets, or use a Sketchfab CC0 model — pick what loads fastest and looks credible at typical zoom levels.

Approach:

1. Source/bundle a single low-poly satellite glTF (~50-200KB). Place at public/assets/satellite.glb. If a free Cesium-hosted model is more reliable, use that URL directly.

2. SatelliteLayer LOD strategy:
   - Camera altitude > 5,000,000 m (5000 km) → render as current colored Point (1px) by category. Same color scheme as today.
   - Camera altitude ≤ 5,000,000 m → render top N closest satellites (cap at 200) as glTF Entity with model: { uri: '/assets/satellite.glb', minimumPixelSize: 32, maximumScale: 50000 }. Rest stay as points.
   - Re-evaluate LOD on camera move-end (not every frame). Throttle to 500ms.

3. glTF orientation:
   - Each rendered glTF satellite gets an OrientationProperty pointing nadir (toward Earth center). Use Cesium.Transforms.headingPitchRollQuaternion or a VelocityOrientationProperty if simpler.

4. Click handling:
   - glTF satellites must remain pickable through the central LEFT_CLICK pick resolver from P11. Same SelectedEntity{type:'satellite',...} payload as before.

5. Color preservation:
   - At point-render mode, keep current category coloring (LEO/MEO/GEO/etc). At glTF mode, no per-satellite tinting needed — model material is fine.

6. Performance guardrails:
   - If FPS drops below 50 during glTF mode, log [SAT LOD] fps_drop and reduce glTF cap from 200 to 100.
   - Confirm 15,304 point-mode rendering still hits 165 FPS.

Definition of done:
- Globe-scale view: same colored points as today, no perf regression
- Zoom in below 5000 km: nearest satellites visibly become 3D models, oriented toward Earth
- Click 3D satellite → EntityPanel populates correctly via P11 central pick handler
- FPS stays above 50 in worst case (200 glTF + 15k points + everything else)
- Theater flythroughs that go close to ISS / GEO sats trigger the LOD swap

Do not touch fires, trails, theaters, airspace, or tileset settings.
